SUWDC

The Southern Utah Web Developer Coalition

We are a group of passionate web developers, software engineers, and developer types in Southern Utah looking to network, learn from/teach each other, and build awesome stuff together along the way.

We use Slack/GitHub for most of our communication and Google Hangouts for our video based meet ups.

This means less time commitment and more efficient collaboration.

Interested? Check us out on GitHub and get in touch.

*Wide range of skill levels and experience welcome.
